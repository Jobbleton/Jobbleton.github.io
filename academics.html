Academics

This page will provide an overview to my academic goals and performance by providing three documents. The first two are created by any student starting an Electrical Engineering degree at Iowa State University: The Educational Objective and the General Education Component. These documents are available below, along with the associated descriptions. The final document, my unofficial transcript, is provided at the end of the page.

Educational Objective

Written as part of EE 166 , the Educational Objective explains my short term and long term educational goals. It also correlates how I intend to apply my education in the professional workplace.

Last Updated: Apr 04, 2009

General Education Component

The General Education Component (GEC), also written as part of EE 166, explains what general education classes I planned on taking. In addition to listing the classes, the GEC explains why I chose those classes and how I intend to apply the knowledge I gain from them to my professional career.

Last Updated: Apr 04, 2009

Unofficial Transcript

This transcript contains a chronological list of all courses taken at Iowa State University, along with the grades attained in those classes. In addition to the class list, the transcript contains a listing of any accepted transfer classes.

The official transcript can be provided upon request.

Last Updated: Oct 16, 2009
