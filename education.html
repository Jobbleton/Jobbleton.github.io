Specific Takeaways

Over all my technical classes, I learned the importance of models and modularity on systems of any size. I discovered and used testing tools of all kinds, explored intelligent agents, and focused on efficiency for real-time systems and algorithm design.

A highlight was developing sensors on a Roomba-type robot in C: our final project involved land surveying with limited echolocation, which taught me the importance of caution and analysis in environments with limited information.

Beyond engineering, my interest in economics and finance is deep-rooted. I value the understanding of how geopolitical and temporal consequences affect the work we do in the engineering field.
